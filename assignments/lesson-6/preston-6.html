Comes Home a Champion

by Teresa Chipman

In Preston the sport of bowling is alive and well as evidenced by success of a Preston team at the 2019 Idaho State Tournament. It was held at Meridian Lanes in Meridian for four weekends - April 13 through May 5 - where over 200 teams participated. The Wright Angle team comprised of Bif Wright, Jeremy Wright, Nils Olsen, and Alex Inglet performed in a winning way. They brought home to Pop-n-Pins Lanes what may be the first ever team championship for Preston after their scores from the first week of the competition held up and secured them the title.
