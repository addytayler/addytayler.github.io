Thank you and welcome!

We would like to start by thanking you for considering Temple Inn & Suites as the one to host your stay. we are happy to have you and are thrilled you have chosen to stay in a location so close to a house of the Lord. Because we are so near this wonderful house of The Lord we pride ourselves in being a clean, reverant, safe environment. We have a no drug, alcohol, tobacco, coffee, or tea policy. And we strive to follow all guidelines set by the Church of Christ, we welcome you.
